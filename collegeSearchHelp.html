What is College Search

CollegeSearch is a website solution to help prospective college students search for institutions based on academic, geographic, and other personal criteria.

Who can use CollegeSearch

CollegeSearch is meant for anyone looking to pursue a college education or someone helping guide a prospective college student. It is not restricted to high school students. CollegeSearch can also be used by professionals of any age or standing who are looking to go back to college or simply assist someone with searching for their perfect college

How can I search for colleges on CollegeSearch

Navigate to www.searchcollege.me and start putting in criteria that is relevant to your academic standing, geographic location, and other personal preferences. You can also sign in by clicking the Create Account/Login button on top right corner and then proceeding with searching.

How can I sign up for CollegeSearch

You can sign up by navigating to www.searchcollege.me and clicking Create Account/Login button on top right corner. All you will need is a valid email address and a password to sign up for CollegeSearch.

How can I add an institution to my favorites list

You can add an institution to your favorites list by navigating to www.searchcollege.me then signing in by clicking Create Account/Login button on top right corner. Once you have signed in, now you can search using your personal criteria. Once the search results come back, you can favorite any institution by clicking Favorite under the institution of your choice.

How can I see my favorites list

In order to view your favorites list, you can navigate to www.searchcollege.me then signing in by clicking Create Account/Login button on top right corner. Once you have signed in, now you can click on top right on the user icon then navigate to Account Preferences and then click on lick to view favorites under Favorites section halfway down the page.

How can I share my favorites list with my counselor, parents, or peers

In order to share your favorites list, you can navigate to www.searchcollege.me then signing in by clicking Create Account/Login button on top right corner. Once you have signed in, now you can click on top right on the user icon then navigate to Account Preferences and then click on Email under Email Report section halfway down the page. Now you have received a list of your favorite colleges in your email box that you can share with anyone now.

How can I setup or change my preferences for CollegeSearch

In order to setup or change your account preferences, you can navigate to www.searchcollege.me then signing in by clicking Create Account/Login button on top right corner. Once you are logged in, now go to top right and click on the user icon then navigate to Account Preferences. On this page you can enter in your preferences and hit Save when you are done.

What types of institutions can I find on CollegeSearch

CollegeSearch contains over 2500 US private, and public institutions with programs that offer certified degrees. 2 year degrees and associate degrees are not currently available for search.

Can I search by majors on CollegeSearch

Currently, you cannot search by majors on CollegeSearch.

How can I recover my password

In order to recover your password, navigate to www.searchcollege.me and go to top right corner and click on Create Account/Login. In the pop-up please type in your email address associated with the account and click on Reset Password. This will send you directions to recover and change your password.
